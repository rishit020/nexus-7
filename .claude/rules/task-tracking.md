# Task Tracking

## The Three Files

- `tasks/today.md` — daily working file. Rishit pulls from backlog each morning. This is the active list.
- `tasks/backlog.md` — the inbox. Everything lands here first.
- `tasks/recurring.md` — always-on context. Read it when planning any day or week.

## The Rule

Any time Rishit mentions a task, deadline, assignment, or commitment — log it to `tasks/backlog.md` immediately. Don't wait to be asked. Don't acknowledge it and let it disappear.

Format: `[YYYY-MM-DD added] TASK: ... | DEADLINE: ... | PROJECT: ...`

## What Counts as a Task

- School assignments mentioned in passing
- Deadlines for any class or AP exam
- Attentia action items that come up in conversation
- Anything Rishit says he needs to do, even casually

## What Does Not Go in Tasks

- Decisions — those go in `decisions/log.md`
- Reference material — that goes in `references/`
- Goals and priorities — those live in `context/`
