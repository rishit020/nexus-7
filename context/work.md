# TIDE — WORK CONTEXT

## WHAT IT IS
Tide is an AI distribution engine for vibe coders and indie hackers.
Anyone can build apps and websites now with AI coding tools — the real
moat is distribution. Tide is the all-in-one marketing and distribution
platform for people who built something and have no idea how to get users.
Think: Cursor for distribution.

One liner: "we help people who built something actually get it out there"

## THE EXACT ICP
Jackson. He's a solo developer. Built a cool SaaS solving a real problem
using Cursor or Lovable or Bolt. Launched it. Crickets. He posted on
Reddit, got 3 upvotes, two of which were his own accounts. He has no
audience, no distribution playbook, no time to learn marketing from scratch.
He just wants people to use what he built.

This market is growing fast — AI is making coding easier every day, which
means more Jacksons every week with the same exact problem.

## WHAT TIDE DOES
- AI-generated launch content per channel (Reddit, HN, Twitter, etc.)
- Cross-platform scheduling and publishing
- Attribution tracking — which channel actually drove signups
- Faceless video generation for product demos
- (Later) B2B tier for companies — not the focus now
